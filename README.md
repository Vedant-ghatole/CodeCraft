The Mental Health Companion is a smart chatbot that helps users manage stress and improve well-being. It offers guided meditation, breathing exercises, and mood tracking, along with personalized tips based on user emotions. Easy to use and always available, it provides a simple, private, and effective way to support mental health anytime, anywhere.

[Key Features](#key-features)

✅ Guided Meditation – Relax with easy-to-follow meditation sessions.

✅ Breathing Exercises – Simple techniques to reduce stress and stay calm.

✅ Mood Tracking – Keep a daily log of your emotions and track changes.

✅ Personalized Tips – Get helpful advice based on your mood and activities.

✅ Conversational Chatbot Support – Friendly and easy-to-use conversation for mental wellness.

✅ 24/7 Accessibility – Access support whenever you need it.


[Future Enhancements](#future-enhancements)  


🚀 Future Enhancements:


1. Smarter & More Personalized :-

The chatbot can learn from your past responses to give better suggestions.
It can remind you to meditate or breathe when you seem stressed.


2. Talk & Listen Features :-

You can talk to the chatbot instead of typing.
It can understand emotions from your voice and suggest calming activities.


3. Connect with Smart Devices :-

It can link to smartwatches to check your heart rate and stress levels.
If your heart rate is high, it can suggest a quick breathing exercise.


4. Easy to Use Anywhere:-

Supports different languages so more people can use it.
Works on phones, computers, and smart speakers.
